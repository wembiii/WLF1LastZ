Combat Development

1. Ways to obtain hero fragments

  • Danger lurks: 1 orange fragment , 5 purple fragments
  • Full preparedness: 2 orange fragments , 7 purple fragments
  • Weekly gift rewards: Send 10 flowers weekly → claim 6 orange fragments (Profile > Gift Box icon).
  • Trade trucks: 4~8 orange fragments daily.
  • Glory shop: 10 orange fragments weekly.
  • Saga zombies - level 9: 24 orange fragments weekly.
  • Camellia's shop: 25 orange fragments monthly.

2. Combat research in order

  1. Max out elite troops
  2. Max out hero training
  3. After that max out military strategies
  4. Peace shield
  5. Seige to seize

3. Orange Equipments

Ways to obtain:

  • Glory shop: 1 equipment box weekly.
  • Bullseye booty: 12th round chest (1 equipment monthly).
IMPORTANT: Always pour power cores / enhancement alloys into upgrading your orange equipments to level 20 first → then level 50.
Star Orbs: Once you reach level 20, use power cores to improve power. Make sure to complete the first star orb in ALL equipments before going for the 2nd star.
